### General Information / Behind the scenes.

This map was a challenge but a fun one, my close friend Jason gifted me Aseprite a while back and i practiced 3 drawings (they are actually included as an easter egg secret with a gameplay reward of course), i gave it a pause and when this jam was announced i started mocking up ideas.

The name comes up from the Doom map "Killer Colours" from the wad "Alien Vendetta" (Map 31), said map uses pure colors for all the sectors... instead of a remake i made my own map, i wanted to name it in Japanese using Romaji ( the Japanese alphabet but in Latin alphabet), due to limitations i named it in english how it would sound in Romaji... if you pronounce it the way it sounds in japanese "Kira Karaz" it sounds Killer Colors and in Neon since... It's Chumastic and A E S T H E T I C (in the best way i could do it, of course)

The textures or well the materials i knew they weren't gonna be strongly elaborated, but for a while to keep leveling up my gamedev skills i wanted to do some 2D and 3D art, the 2D art part was done in this map... As you may know i like Neon Aesthetic colors, and i proceeded to create textures as i needed with the skill and time i had to do it.

I did most of the recolors in Wally to save time... my base workflow was : Create floor, ceiling, and buttons as suggested, then i went for the neon lights in 16x16 and 32x32 grid, then i proceed to create a sky (not perfect but functional), and other textures as Lights... In the meantime i added my 3 first Sprite arts and recolored them to make them fit the Quake palette... The monkey face and the siren were the ones i retouched the colors mostly... the "Sands of time" was pretty much the same as i imported it into the Quake palette.

Hope you enjoyed my map and had fun, thank you Rabbit and wonz for leading this jam, it was a fun experience.

Hugs and best wishes for everyone, excelsior!
